About

I am a third-year Biomedical Engineering undergraduate at the Department of Electronic & Telecommunication Engineering, University of Moratuwa. I focus on applying mathematics, physiological modeling, biosignal processing, and machine learning to build accessible medical technologies. My experience spans analog & embedded hardware, algorithm design, and user-centered interfaces across collaborative team projects.
