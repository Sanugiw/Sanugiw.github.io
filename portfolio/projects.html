Selected Projects

X-Hale – Portable Spirometer

A smart, portable spirometer designed to make respiratory health monitoring affordable and accessible. Measures airflow velocity and computes FVC and FEV1, with mobile-based visualization for tracking lung health.

Focus: Medical instrumentation, embedded systems, human-centered design

ML-Integrated Table-Mounted Fundoscope

A stationary retinal imaging system that reduces motion artifacts and enables machine learning–based classification of diabetic retinopathy severity to support early screening.

Focus: Medical imaging, machine learning, diagnostic support

Deep Learning for ECG Classification

Developed a deep learning pipeline for automated arrhythmia classification using annotated ECG datasets, exploring feature learning and temporal modeling of cardiac signals.

Focus: Biosignal processing, deep learning
