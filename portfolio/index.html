About

I am a third-year Biomedical Engineering undergraduate at the University of Moratuwa with a strong interest in biosignal processing, medical instrumentation, and machine learning for healthcare.

My work focuses on designing intelligent, human-centered biomedical systems that integrate hardware, software, and data-driven methods to improve diagnostic accessibility and reliability.

Alongside my technical interests, I value clear communication, teamwork, and thoughtful problem-solving, supported by a genuine interest in reading and writing.
